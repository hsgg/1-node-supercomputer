Compiling Evince on Archlinux

So, took me a while to figure out why Evince wouldn't compile. autogen.sh gave me an error:

error: HAVE_INTROSPECTION does not appear in AM_CONDITIONAL

To solve it, install the package gobject-introspectiron. Yes, you need that package even if you are compiling Evince without GObject introspectrion support.
